# Mango Salsa

## Ingredients
- 2 large mangos
- Red onion
- 1 tbsp salt
- 2 red bellpeppers
- 3 habaneros
- 3 tbs lime juice
- Cilantro

## Instructions
1. Small dice mangos, onion, and bellpeppers into equal sizes
2. Mince habaneros, and combine with other ingredients and salt, lime, cilantro
3. Rest for at least 12 hours
